Welcome to Hoya Bloom Photos! Here you can browse through a catalog of stunning images of the exotic blooms of the Hoya plant.

Hoya plants, also known as wax plants or porcelain flowers, are a type of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia and Australia. They are popular among houseplant
enthusiasts for their attractive and unique foliage and clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers.
